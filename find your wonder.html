Motion Design

Disney Find Your Wonder Serie

Serie of webisodes the Disney Princess 'Find Your Wonder' initiative, designed to inspire children to explore new interests and ignite their passions. The project featured captivating Wonder Webisodes starring notable personalities like Helen Skelton, Alice Dearing, and Bebe Vio.

Responsible for postproduction of seven episodes, strategically streamed on Disney's YouTube channel across the Europe and Americas. The production phase involved editing insightful interviews, precise color grading, and the integration of animated typography that seamlessly complemented the video content. The comprehensive process included the crucial steps of localization and distribution, ensuring widespread reach and resonance across all Disney's YouTube channels in the EMEA region.
