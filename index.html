About

Hi, I'm Alex

Creative and motion designer based in London, with 5+ years delivering high-impact work for Warner Bros. Discovery, The Walt Disney Company, and the Financial Times. I specialise in 2D/3D motion graphics, broadcast post-production, and XR/AR development — combining rigorous technical execution with bold visual storytelling.

I hold an MA in Virtual & Augmented Reality from Goldsmiths, and have shipped AR experiences for institutions including the Tate Museum and Snap. I pride myself on pushing the boundaries of what's possible at the intersection of technology and art — creating experiences that are truly unforgettable.

I'm always open to ambitious new projects. Please feel free to get in touch.
