About

Hi, I'm Alex

Creative designer based in London, UK. Over the years, I've had the privilege of working with a variety of high-profile clients, including Financial Times Live, The Walt Disney Company, Lego, and many more. I pride myself on thinking outside of the box to deliver projects that are both visually stunning and technically advanced. My goal is to push the boundaries of what's possible in the world technology and art, and to create experiences that are truly unforgettable.

Please feel free to get in touch! I am always open for new opportunities and exciting projects.
