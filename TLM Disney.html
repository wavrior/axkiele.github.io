Graphic and Motion Design

The Little Mermaid Campaign

Led a collaborative effort with a team of designers to orchestrate a comprehensive marketing campaign promoting toys and clothing tied to the movie premiere of The Little Mermaid across the EMEA region. The project encompassed crafting promotional assets for Amazon and Fire TV, including banners and animated end-cards, strategically aligning with the film's release.


Engaged with prominent clients such as Lego and Mattel to effectively promote their products within The Little Mermaid brand context, ensuring a cohesive and impactful campaign. Additionally, played a key role in editing and localizing The Little Mermaid Disney+ advertisement, strategically employed to boost the movie's premiere on the Disney+ platform throughout Europe.

This project exemplifies my adeptness in collaborative project management, strategic partnerships with renowned brands, and the successful execution of multimedia campaigns on a global scale.
