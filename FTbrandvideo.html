Financial Times · Motion Design · 2022

FTLive Brand Video

Client
Financial Times
Scope
Motion Graphics, Video Production
Tools
After Effects, Premiere Pro
Year
2022 — ongoing

Spearheaded the creation of the FTLive Brand Video — a dynamic showcase encapsulating pivotal moments from Financial Times events, used as a powerful brand introduction at each conference. Responsibilities extended to designing header videos for event websites, and creating targeted post-production marketing videos promoting specific events across social media and web. The project demanded a strong editorial sensibility aligned with the Financial Times' reputation for quality and rigour.
