Graphic and Motion Design

Disney Wish Campaign

Working on EMEA Marketing Campaign for Disney's Christmas festivities, intricately intertwined with the promotion of the Wish movie. Collaborating with a team of designers and producers, the project encompassed the creation of diverse marketing assets to amplify the holiday and cinematic experience.

Tasks included the development of promotional assets for Amazon and Freeve, strategically crafting Fire TV Landing Page elements, and producing a dynamic Fire Tablet animation utilized as a Google tablet background. Additionally, multiple products carousels were meticulously designed and implemented across the entire EMEA campaign, ensuring a cohesive and visually engaging brand experience.

This project showcases my expertise in both Motion Design and Graphic Design, demonstrating a seamless integration of creative elements for a comprehensive and impactful marketing campaign within the Disney brand.
